About the Artist

Nicole Murphy (she, her, hers) is a Junior level Graphic Design major enrolled at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. She is 21 years old and is currently located in the New Bedford area of Massachusetts. Nicole is proficient in several Adobe programs including: Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop. During her time at Umass Dartmouth so far, she has completed several design projects including digital media and printed media.
